Scale

Three Levels, Same 7 Sections

Level 1
Individual
1 person · Notion · 5 min/day
  • Goals: what winning looks like this month
  • Outputs: what's in motion, what agents run
  • Progress: today's actions, what's blocked
  • Context: what compounded, new data
  • People: follow-up, interaction log
  • Tools: what's connected, what's friction
  • Shared: what you put out, what came back
Level 2
Team
3–15 people · Discord + Airtable
  • Shared rubrics aligned from individual up
  • Shared wiki: collective knowledge, findable
  • Shared progress: who's building what
  • Exchange: one breakthrough feeds everyone
  • Team lead queries patterns and blockers
  • Private stays private. Team owns aggregation only.
Level 3
School / Org
30+ people · Principal queries brain
  • Rubrics flow down: org defines, dept adapts
  • Context flows up: individual to org
  • Principal: what's blocked, what's improving
  • Dept head: vertical view, teacher progress
  • Teacher: students, growth evidence
  • EDB/board: living data, not reports
